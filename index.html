I was wrong, completely wrong.

I was angry — blindly, stupidly angry — and instead of talking to you like you deserved, I chose to go cold. I ignored you. Entirely. Deliberately. And I knew, even while I was doing it, how badly it must have hurt you.

That was one of the worst things I have ever done. Not because anger is wrong — but because I turned it into a wall between us and left you on the other side of it, alone, wondering what you did wrong. You did nothing wrong. This was all me.

I am ashamed. Genuinely, deeply ashamed of myself for treating someone I love that way.

Sorry. So sorry. Truly sorry.

What you mean to me, every single day

You are my first thought when I wake up. Before the world rushes in, before anything else becomes real — it is you. Your laugh, your face, the small ways you exist that I have memorised without trying.

"She is the first thought of the morning and the last thought of every night. Always. Without fail."

I love you entirely — not the idea of you, not the version of you that is always happy with me — all of you. Your softness, your strength, the way you hold onto things that matter. I carry you with me through every ordinary moment of the day, and somehow that makes even the dull parts of life feel warmer.

You are not just someone I love. You are the person I think about when I imagine what tomorrow should look like. And I nearly threw a piece of that away with my silence, with my pride, with my foolish anger. I will never stop being sorry for it.

♡ ♡ ♡
🌹

I promise you — never again.

I am not asking for forgiveness because I deserve it. I am asking because I want the chance to be better — and I will be, every single day, for as long as you let me.

  • I will never go cold on you again. If I am angry, I will say so — with words, not walls.
  • I will never make you feel invisible, unheard, or unwanted. You are everything but those things.
  • If you choose to give me a punishment — any punishment — I accept it fully and without protest. You have that right.
  • I will talk to you. Even when it is hard. Especially when it is hard.
  • I will remind you, on the ordinary days and the difficult ones, that you are loved beyond what words can do justice to.

This is not just something I am saying to make things okay right now. This is a promise I intend to keep for every day after this one.
